Lovejoy’s music has been described as indie rock, alt-rock, and pop-punk. Some of the band’s influences include, Los Campesinos!, The Strokes, Crywank, Arctic Monkeys, Bo Burnham, The Killers, My Chemical Romance, and The Front Bottoms. In a 2023 interview with MTV, the band described their music as, “Aromantic British Noises”.
